Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. Here's how our refund process works.

We at www.manovikasa.com want you to be satisfied with your purchase of any of our products. If you have any questions or problems, please let us know by contacting our support team directly at support@manovikasa.com.

How Refunds Work

If you couldn't get the desired results with any of our products, contact our support team. Show us you really took every necessary step by presenting ALL the work that you completed. We will review it and endeavour to help resolve the issue. If you have taken the necessary steps and are not getting results, we will work with you one-on-one to help you achieve results. If we are unable to help, we will issue a FULL refund.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, you must submit your request and completed homework before 11:59 PM Indian Standard Time on the 60th day following the end of the particular program. After you submit your materials, all refunds are discretionary*.

No refunds will be provided more than 60 days following the date of end of the particular program. There will be no exceptions.

Payment Plans

If you opted for a payment plan, all payments must be completed. You may not cancel part way through. This is done to avoid fraud.

If the payment plan is not completed on time, we will pursue it with a collection agency until the entire payment plan is made. Additional fees and fines might apply in this case.

Full Payment & Course Completion

If you pay in full or choose the payment plan and take the course and implement the strategy, then there will be no refunds issued and you must complete the payment plan if you selected one.

*Note: All refunds are discretionary. If you just downloaded the training material (PDFs, audios, videos, etc.) and then promptly asked for a refund, we reserve the right to deny your refund request. The purpose of this policy is to allow individuals to attempt the framework, and if it doesn't work, they can recover the amount they invested. It was not intended to allow individuals to take the training material and then request a refund a few days later.

Furthermore, if it is clear that your homework/tasks are incomplete or that you did not implement the strategies taught in the course, your refund will be denied. However, we will still help and support you in order to get you results.

By purchasing, downloading or viewing any of our products you are also agreeing with our refund policy and terms & conditions.
